Our Story

Founded in 2020, Why So Cheesy??? was born from a simple idea: to create delicious, authentic Jain food that doesn't compromise on taste or tradition.

Our chefs combine traditional Jain cooking methods with modern culinary techniques to create cheesy masterpieces that are 100% vegetarian and follow all Jain dietary principles.

We use only the freshest ingredients, locally sourced vegetables, and authentic spices to ensure every bite is bursting with flavor and made with love.
